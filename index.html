WEDDING CEREMONY

WHEN AND WHERE
Our wedding ceremony will be at Oleana, a small restaurant in Cambridge, Massachusetts. Cambridge, is a vibrant, historic city just across the river from Boston.
DATE & LOCATION

Saturday August 22, 2015 at Oleana, 134 Hampshire St, Cambridge, MA.

TIME

12:00-4pm. Ceremony at 12:30pm, Reception and Lunch to follow.

ACCOMODATION

We have not yet made a list of accomodation. There are many hotels in Cambridge and Boston (AirBnB is also a good option).
