THINGS TO DO

IN BOSTON

Here are a few of the many sights within and around Boston.

The Freedom Tail

A 2.5 mile walk past many of Boston's historic sites. Starts in Boston Common goes through the North End and ends at the Bunker Hill Monument in Charlestown. The length of the entire trail is marked by a red line.

The North End

The North End is Boston's oldest residential neighborhoods. It is an Italian-American neighborhood with many restaurants. Mike's Pastry is a popular spot to get cannoli for locals and tourists alike. Other major sites include Paul Revere house and the Old North Church.

Isabella Stewart Gardner Museum

An impressive art collection housed in the Isabella Stewart Gardner's former mansion next to the Back Bay Fens.

Beacon Hill

An historic neighborhood next to Boston Common and the Massachusetts State House.

Fenway Park

The oldest ball park in Major League Baseball and the home of the Boston Red Sox.

Minute Man National Historic Park

A beautiful park at the site where the American Revolutionary War began. Located just outside of Boston near Lexington and Concord.
