ACCOMODATION

WHERE TO STAY

There are many hotels in Boston and Cambridge and there is no official hotel for our wedding. We suggest a few hotels below and we are looking into whether any of them offer discounted rates for wedding parties.

AirBNB and VRBO have many good options in Boston/Cambridge. Some of these are more affordable, particularly when shared by multiple people.

Kendall Hotel

350 Main Street, Cambridge MA | 866-566-1300 | $$

The Kendall Hotel is located by MIT. It is next to the Kendall Square T station, which makes it easy to travel into Boston. It is a 0.8 mile walk or cab ride to the wedding venue.

Courtyard Marriot Cambridge

777 Memorial Drive Cambridge, MA | 617-492-7777 | $$

The Courtyard Marriot is next to the river and is only 0.2 miles from our house. It is a 0.8 mile walk to the Central Square T station and a 1.4 mile walk or cab ride to the wedding venue.

Sheraton Commander

16 Garden Street, Cambridge, MA | 617-547-4800 | $$

The Sheraton Commander is next to Cambridge Common. It is 1.5 miles to the wedding venue and 0.4 miles to the Harvard Square T Station.

Charles Hotel

1 Bennett St, Cambridge, MA | 800-882-1818 | $$$$

The Charles Hotel is an upscale hotel next to Harvard Square. It is 0.2 miles to the Harvard Square T Station and 1.6 miles from the wedding venue.
